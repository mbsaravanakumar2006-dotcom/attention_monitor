About the Project

Our Classroom Engagement Monitor uses state-of-the-art computer vision and deep learning models to provide educators with a clear understanding of student participation. By analyzing visual cues like head pose, eye contact, and body language, the system helps identify when students are most engaged and when they might need extra support or a change in teaching style.

This project aims to bridge the gap between traditional teaching and modern instructional needs, ensuring no student is left behind due to unnoticed disengagement.
